I Am Groot!

Meet Groot, the Flora Colossus from Planet X and a member of the Guardians of the Galaxy!

About Groot

Groot is a sentient, tree-like being with incredible strength, regenerative abilities, and a vocabulary that consists mostly of "I am Groot." Despite his limited speech, Groot is known for his loyalty, courage, and willingness to protect his friends at all costs. As a key member of the Guardians of the Galaxy, Groot has captured the hearts of Marvel fans worldwide with his unique charm and character.
